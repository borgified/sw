Night time

You awaken from your deep slumber but it is still dark outside. Thankfully the life support system is still functioning and you have managed to get the emergency lights to turn on. Unfortunately, turning on the lights was a bad idea. All sorts of critters, drawn by the illumination gathered around your ship and threatened to get inside from the vent slits. You hope that by daylight, they will be gone. As you try to stretch, you find that your bones have gotten stiff and your muscles cramped. Seeing that there were still a good couple of hours to go before dawn, you decide to go back to sleep ... BUT you realized that you hadn't eaten anything for the past 24 hours and you begin wondering if it was possible to hunt down a rabbit or something to roast.

Danger Ahead!

Forging on, you move with undaunted determination. If there was a way out of this dump, you were going to find it. There are few JediKnights with your level of resolution but, then again, it was the same stubbornness that got you here in the first place, when you insisted that you had enough fuel to make the trip to Aldeaaran.

Just then, you felt a disturbance in the Force. Up ahead, there was something. But you decide to ignore it and continue picking up firewood for tonight.

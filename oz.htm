You Go!, Girl.

You awaken from your nightmare and find yourself back in the farmland with Uncle Owen. You remember that you had to go with your uncle to buy some droids tomorrow and that you hadn't finished your homework yet.

Furthermore, you realize that you are a girl. And that you, in fact, do not have an uncle named Owen. Your dog, Toto, barks happily seeing you awake and you live out the rest of your life in many adventures in the Wonderful Land of Oz.

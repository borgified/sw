The Darkness

Up ahead, illuminated by the bluish glow of the lightsaber, looked like the end of the cave. Upon closer inspection, you see an arrow carved on a large boulder, pointing down. No sooner than you had anticipated, both a sinking feeling of being caught in a deathtrap AND the realization that you were actually sinking into the moss-covered quicksand, stabbed an icy shard of fear into your heart.

You instinctively tried to levitate yourself out of trouble, as you have always done before, but realize now that your powers have completely vanished. Knee-deep into the sand, your struggle only quickened your descent and the frustration of realizing the uselessness of a lightsaber at this point, was killing you.

You begin to laugh. As desperate as the situation looked, there was really nothing else to be done.
